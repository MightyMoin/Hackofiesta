About

Hack o fiesta is a student-held hackathon brought to you by Axios, The Technical Club of IIIT Lucknow. We enable and encourage budding as well as seasoned programmers to build usable and functional software, a finished product that can aid individuals and corporations alike.

This hackathon strives to explore the endless potential of technology and its creators. You would be amazed at what a handful of programmers can do, given a computer system and Three days.

FAQ

Many a times it happens that what we are doubtful about, has been the same for someone else and hence already answered. Please find below a list of such questions and their answers:

  • arrow_drop_downWhat is a hackathon?
    A hackathon is a marathon-themed event for student developers, where they work collectively on a project. The projects are usually based on a theme decided by the organisers beforehand, and are made to solve real world problems.
  • arrow_drop_downWho can attend?
    Beginners, pros, literally everyone. Anyone with a passion for learning, or primarily, undergraduate programmers are welcome to attend. Even if you can’t code, we can teach you or you can help out in the hackathon.
  • arrow_drop_downHow can I help?
    We appreciate all who want to support our cause and we definitely need your awesome skills. Even if someone can’t code, volunteers can support the organizing team during the hackathon with various tasks. It’s always good to have a helping hand.
  • arrow_drop_downWhy Social Good?
    Well, why the bug not? It is to show gratitude and help out people who don’t program, by building useful software for them. It’s high time to give back to society, and this hackathon is the perfect opportunity for that.
  • arrow_drop_downWhat can I build?
    Possibly something that resonates with the theme of the event, that is social good. It can be a website, software, an applet or maybe even a piece of hardware. We’d love to see anything that solves a social problem or goes with our agenda.
  • arrow_drop_downWhat if I don’t have a team or an Idea?
    Even if you don’t have a team or an idea, that's totally fine! We'll have a team forming event right before development starts. We’ll also have an ideation event where everyone will be brainstorming to generate new ideas.
